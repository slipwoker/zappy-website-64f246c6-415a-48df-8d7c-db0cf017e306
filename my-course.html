What's Inside the Full Program:

Get the complete 3-part course – $299 and begin your journey to your true self.

I'm Ariel Boton, an author who shares my life story exactly as it is — painful, brave, and honest. I was born in Turkey and raised in Israel, and have walked a life path filled with challenges and triumphs. I experienced domestic violence, discovered my identity as a transgender person, and through the pain and struggle, found the desire to share, illuminate, and empower.

In my writing, I blend directness with sensitivity, humor with raw honesty, to shine light on situations that usually remain hidden in the shadows. My books and courses are my way of giving you a voice, hope, and real tools for growth and true healing.

They're not just stories — they're reflections of courage, survival, and rebirth. A reminder that no matter what you've been through, you can rise, heal, and begin again.

This course is a mental and emotional journey that guides transgender women toward a reality where they can feel free, safe, and accepting of themselves. Through practical tools and a unique theatrical experience, you'll gain inspiration and guidance for a life filled with meaning and joy.
